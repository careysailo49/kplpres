Zirna leh hnathawhna lam :

Upa Vanlalmuana hi Primary School a\anga High School thleng N. Bukpui-ah kalin, Bukpui P/S II-ah te, Gov’t M/S Bukpuiah te, Gov’t CH.K.M H/S Bukpuiah te a lût \hin a. College chu Hrangbana College-ah kalin PUC a zo ta lo a ni. Kum 1986-ah ITI-ah Carpentry a training a, kum 1987 a\anga 1988 chhungin ITI-ah Officiating Instructor (Carpentry) a thawk a. Kum 1993 November ni 8-ah W.E. Teacher hna hmuin Gov’t Saiphai Middle School-ah a thawk \an a, kum 2003 October a\anga 2024 September ni 18 thlengin Gov’t M/S Thingdawlah thawk lehin, ni 19 September, 2024 a\angin Gov’t Khianga M/S-ah a thawk leh a ni.

Rawngbawlna lam:

Upa Vanlalmuana hi piantirh a\anga a nupui neih thlengin Salvation Army-a lawi \hin a ni a. A nupui neih a\angin Presbyterian Kohhranah a rawn lawi lût a. Salvation Army-a a lawi lai hian an khua Bukpui-ah Naupang Sunday School leh an \halai pawl SAY lamah te theih tawpa rawng lo bawl \hin a ni. Nupui a neih hnua Presbyterian Kohhran lama a lawi hnu pawhin Senior Department Zirtirtuah te \angin theih tawpin rawng a bawl a. Kum 2002 a\anga 2005 chhung hian Salvation Army lamah awm lehin Diakkawn Corp-ah a lawi leh ren rawn a; mahse, kum 2006 a\angin New Diakkawn Kohranah Prebyterian-a awm turin a rawn inpe leh a ni. New Diakkawn Kohhranah hian Puitling Sunday School Zirtirtuah te \angin awm hmun sawn avangin |umpui Presbyterian Kohhran lamah te pawh a lawi leh a. Kum 2007 October ni 8-ah anmahni in thar Khuangpuilamah rawn inbengbelin Vengthar Presbyterian Kohhranah a lawi leh a. Kum 2008-ah Upate Puitu atan ruat a ni a, Khuangpuilam Kohhran indanna tura Building Sub-committee atan ruat a ni bawk. January ni 10, 2010-a Khuangpuilam Kohhran a indan khan Adhoc Committee atana ruat niin Treasurer-ah ruat a ni a. Kum 2013 a\anga 2016 thleng Tual Upa atan thlan a ni a. Kum 2017-ah Tual Upa atana thlan nawn leh niin kum 2020-ah Kolasib Venglai Kohhran Biak Inah Rev. David Sai\huama Sailo-in Presbyterian Upa atan a nemnghet (Ordain) a ni.
